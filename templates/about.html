Just a brief history of my ascension to developer

In the spring of my career

As a young man new to the world of data, I had a lot to learn. Starting by uploading products onto the site, I quickly learnt how to create processes that helped me be more efficient in not only creating products but also assisting customers and suppliers effortlessly which then caught the attention of the head of catalogue...

Thus the summer of data had begun

Once it became evident that I could provide solutions to issues that were never seen before, i was tasked with solving the issues plaguing our intake of stock which gave me a deeper understanding of how the different aspects of the business could be affected by data. At this time I was working with the Catalogue department to monitor and correct the data being used and the supply chain department to assist with the intake of problematic stock...

An autumn tale

Once in the role of "fixer", I quickly upskilled myself by using advanced excel and MySQL to generate my own reports that provided me with more insight than I was getting from the teams I was assisting. Because of that,I was able to start utilizing the data and begin my own data projects. At that time, I was also training a team of three specialist who were hired to replace in an operational capacity so that I could focus on "higher level issues"...

Winter has come

My winter started in supply chain assisting the Division lead of the admin department with creating automations and reports while doing ad-hoc tasks. That is where I discovered my interest in Visual basic which has lead us here.
